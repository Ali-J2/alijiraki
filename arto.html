Arto

Arto is an action packed hack and slash game. You travel to different worlds, each featuring a distinct art style.

Arto on Steam

Development

Arto was the first game created at Orion Games. I joined the team with the project already well underway. Initially my responsibilities were to program environmental interactions (things that would come to life once they are colored in, interactables, characters and dialog). Eventually my responsibilities grew to creating a deep combat system, enemy/boss AI, making cutscenes, and tweaking game feel.

Six Art Styles

Each style has its own unique themes, enemies, and camera effects.

Combat System

Arto features a deep combat system that includes combos, parrying, blocking, different weapons, and many unique bosses and enemy AIs.

Cutscenes

This is the first cutscene in the game, where the protagonist (Liv) finds her first weapon. Each boss is introduced with a cutscene as well.

Paint Magic System

Arto includes a complex paint magic system that allows players to configure the type of projectile/magic they wish to create, up to over 60 unique combinations.

The Hub

This is where worlds are unlocked and levels can be entered. Canvases show a preview of what the world is.
