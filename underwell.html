Dungeons of Legend: Underwell

Dungeons of Legend: Underwell is a first person grid based dungeon crawling adventure. It is inspired by modern classics like Legend of Grimrock as well as older classics like Eye of the Beholder, Dungeon Master and Lands of Lore.

Dungeons of Legend: Underwell on Google Play

Development

Dungeons of Legend: Underwell was the first game I've released. I had to make most of the 2D assets from scratch using my limited pixel art skills as well as program the grid based movement, AI systems and puzzles. It was a fairly ambitious project for a first release, but I loved the Legend of Grimrock games when they first came out and wanted to make something similar.

Character Classes

The Dungeons of Legend games featured character classes that can be chosen at the start of the game. Each class offered a playstyle and movesets that were unique to them.

Pixel Art Graphics

A lot of the enemy sprites were hand drawn and animated. It's programmer art at the end of the day, but hey, it got the job done fairly well.

Puzzles and Traps

In this room, the player had to choose the 2 guilty choices out of 6 guillotines. The wrong answer could lead to painful consequences!

Magic System

The Wizard class was the only class that could use magic. Writing some of these descriptions was good fun.
